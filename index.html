Our Program

This two-year Ontario College Diploma program is designed to prepare students for a variety of careers in the digital media industry. Students explore the primary elements of media projects planning and development with equal importance given to the creative and the technical aspects. Strong emphasis is placed on hands-on media production through courses in web development, digital imaging, authoring, animation, photography and videography. Students will plan and execute individual and team-based projects and gain practical experience working with real live clients. The curriculum is delivered on a variety of mobile operating systems.

Success Factors

This program is well-suited for students who:

  • Are highly adaptable to today’s technology.
  • Are imaginative and enjoy solving problems.
  • Enjoy a hands-on approach to learning about the multimedia industry.
  • Enjoy working on projects in a team-based environment.

Level 4 Expenses

When students reach Level 4 of the program they will be specializing in either a video or web stream. At this point they will be required to make additional purchases.


Web Stream

Students taking the web development elective courses must purchase their own domain name and web hosting.

Video Stream

Students taking the video elective courses must make additional investments in their equipment:

  • A tripod with a fluid tripod head and quick release plate. (approx. $100 +)
  • Additional Class 10 (10MB/s or faster) MicroSD HC memory cards 64GB or larger for their camera. (approx. $75)
  • Telephoto zoom lens for their Canon camera f4 70 - 200mm or better. (approx. $700 - $1,700)
  • An external flash lighting device. Specifics to be discussed with course professor.
